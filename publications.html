Dohyeon Yeo

I am a Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher in the HCIS Lab. Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate in the College of Information and Computing at Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST), advised by Prof. SeungJun Kim. I received my M.S. in Intelligent Robotics at GIST. Before joining GIST, I received my B.S. in Control and Automation Engineering at Korea Maritime and Ocean University. My research interests include in-vehicle UI/UX and extended reality (XR).

Email: ing.dohyeonyeo [at] gm.gist.ac.kr
